The time has come to knuckle down and finish off my final year report and any straggling projects as well as start revision, it is also that time of year where summer is looming around the corner. We are just starting to get a few snippets of hot weather and so it's nice to enjoy a cold drink. Considering that, as a software engineer, my caffeine level is directly proportionate to my productivity, I decided to take inspiration from an Australian couple who I met during my last weekend holiday.
!http://lh3.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DQGohUsQI/AAAAAAAAo0k/m9RtO9VcB6M/s400/2010-03-27%2014.28.46.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/i4DG3yI76doU_maYERFxiA?feat=embedwebsite
From "Iced Coffee":http://picasaweb.google.com/sphyrz/IcedCoffee?feat=embedwebsite

h4. Ingredients
* Instant coffee (1 - 3 tsp) or espresso (2 - 3 shots)
* Sugar (same quantity as coffee)
* Coca (if you want a Mocha)
* Mug
* Boiling water
* Ice

h4. Instructions
!http://lh5.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DP5iQypvI/AAAAAAAAo0c/GrvzIeeA1BM/s400/2010-03-27%2014.30.06.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/Cryhb2gxKStAbvW19pZ7hA?feat=embedwebsite

Insert your sugar into the mug.

!http://lh4.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DO-ZNi1EI/AAAAAAAAozk/CyHP5lfCTuw/s400/2010-03-27%2014.30.32.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/oLEIbbP2Ao3eKgjX5lxqFA?feat=embedwebsite

Optionally add Coca to make an Iced Mocha.

!http://lh4.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DPiBZ3UfI/AAAAAAAAo0M/X4-LPMj5xIQ/s400/2010-03-27%2014.31.11.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/Dm35JhmQMB7JYkxTzWj_0g?feat=embedwebsite

Add coffee granules or espresso shots.

!http://lh6.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DN3TGciwI/AAAAAAAAozE/AqsNw-p8hPI/s400/2010-03-27%2014.31.52.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/JgghgIyeZemmGCFXRm679A?feat=embedwebsite

If you are using instant coffee then add a tiny amount of hot water. Either way, mix up all of the ingredients.

!http://lh5.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DOXxLRIJI/AAAAAAAAozM/yva5q41NzPk/s400/2010-03-27%2014.34.12.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/uC853X19s6LkrUhf3l-AyA?feat=embedwebsite

If you find that the granules don't mix well, then a short blast in the microwave tends to dissolve everything.

!http://lh3.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DOe1IDL9I/AAAAAAAAozU/0ag1Ahonj9Q/s400/2010-03-27%2014.34.55.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/tEzfCxFEzs1jJkLF8VDMeA?feat=embedwebsite

Add Ice.

!http://lh3.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DO2ezlT2I/AAAAAAAAozc/3PZYRVJT5vM/s400/2010-03-27%2014.35.13.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/Ri-ZebC8_8GoGNRIRdW0-g?feat=embedwebsite

Mix up well to rapidly cool the hot drink.

!http://lh3.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DNosA4NwI/AAAAAAAAoys/b3dddQu4lhw/s400/2010-03-27%2014.35.35.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/7Qko8SseIeMxJrpSFmcfeg?feat=embedwebsite

Check that the drink is ice cold before continuing.

!http://lh5.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DNupdGg0I/AAAAAAAAoy4/Gl0Xp3KoYJs/s400/2010-03-27%2014.36.49.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/qF8uQ8eG-RyJ7HFSu_7ZtA?feat=embedwebsite

Add milk.

!http://lh4.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DNHFOLIyI/AAAAAAAAoyk/dLcDvJX4Plg/s400/2010-03-27%2014.37.16.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/ilqhhoAqYlKG7W15GpDCDg?feat=embedwebsite

Optionally top-up with water.

!http://lh4.ggpht.com/_5pFmgjbK0K0/S7DD3HYbjnI/AAAAAAAAoyM/JtJ7aRe_Xg4/s400/2010-03-27%2014.38.55.jpg!:http://picasaweb.google.com/lh/photo/Attmb9IPDVJAqKLzBmcVMQ?feat=embedwebsite

Enjoy your iced coffee or iced mocha.
